Plan retention tree groups based on ecosystem service values enriched by hotspot analysis (Getis & Ord 1992). The delineation of the retention tree groups is applied from the highest hotspot values of ecosystem services.

Input parameters

  • Trees Trees as vector point format
  • Tree species Field of tree species with codes (1=Scots pine, 2=Norway spruce, 3=Betula pendula, 4= Betula pubenscens,5=Populus tremula, 7=Alnus glutinosa, 29 = other deciduous tree)
  • Tree species Field of diameter (cm)
  • DTW Dept-to-Water -index (m)
  • Vegetation zones Vegetation zones of boreal forest as vector polygon format
  • Water body Water bodies to consider in Phosphorus retention calculation
  • Forest grid Attibute data of forest grid need to have "fertilityclass","MEANHEIGHTPINE","MEANHEIGHTSPRUCE","MEANHEIGHTDECIDUOUS"
  • Retention tree count (trees/ha) Count of retention trees. Minimum of the PEFC sertificate is 10 trees / ha
  • Advanced parameters Weighting of the ecoystem services. Pret emphasize water protection, DTW biodiversity and water protection, Ds biodiversity, and DWP biodiversity and climate

Outputs

  • Trees Single-trees of planning area with ecosystem services values
  • Retention tree groups Proposed retention tree groups based on weight of the indices
